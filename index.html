Welcome To CBIT.

Chaitanya Bharathi Institute of Technology (CBIT), Hyderabad is a prestigious institute established in the year 1979. It is affiliated to Osmania University and has been approved by AICTE. The institute holds reputable status among many popular ranking agencies. It offers B.E/B.Tech at the UG level and ME, M.Tech, MBA, MCA at the PG level.

Academics

The Institute, committed to Education and Innovation, started with three-Degree Courses in Engineering for 200 Students and over the last 42 Years has emerged as a Dream Destination for: Students seeking to excel in Engineering and Management Education, Teaching Community to progress with a rewarding Career and Corporates to source well-rounded Engineers.
